Meet Pawan

the Heart and Hands Behind White House Spa

  • Next

Known affectionately by his Thai nickname, "Pop," Pawan is the proud owner and certified massage therapist at White House Spa in Cha Am. Having grown up in this serene seaside town, Pawan always aspired to build something meaningful for his community. After completing his Bacherlor's degree and earning his qualifications as a massage therapist in Bangkok, he returned home to establish this family-run spa on his family's property.

Pawan combines his expert skills with a deep passion for customer care. He has handpicked a team of the best local massage therapists to ensure every guest enjoys an exceptional and authentic Thai wellness experience.

Though naturally shy, Pawan’s dedication to his craft and his customers speaks volumes. He goes above and beyond to make every visitor feel welcome and at ease.

Beyond the spa, Pawan is football crazy and a devoted Chelsea supporter. He’s also a local guide of sorts, with a wealth of knowledge about his hometown Cha Am and the neighboring Hua Hin, where he plays five-aside and went to school.

Discover the care, expertise, and hospitality that Pawan and his team bring to White House Spa—it’s more than a massage; it’s a heartfelt experience.
