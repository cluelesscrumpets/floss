About Floss

Founded by Phakapohn Paopan DDS (Doctor of Dental Surgery) in 2023, Floss Dental Clinic brings a truly modern, innovative, and a vibrant approach to the dentistry landscape of Pranburi.

We combine cutting-edge technology with a fresh perspective, offering safe, comfortable, and exceptional dental treatments in a welcoming and contemporary environment.

Located past the entrance of Lotus' Pranburi on the left-hand side, a 25-minute drive from Huahin town center, we are experienced in welcoming tourists, ex-pats, and local Thai patients alike for top-class dental treatments.

Please don't hesitate to call into the surgery, or get in touch via phone or email today! We'll be happy to assist in any way we can.
